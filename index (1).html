About Me

Where regulatory rigor meets analytical precision.

I am a Senior AML/BSA Compliance professional with over five years of specialized experience uncovering financial crime across the digital asset and fintech landscape. My work sits at the crossroads of rigorous regulatory compliance and cutting-edge investigative technology.

At Circle — the issuer of USDC and one of the most closely regulated digital asset firms in the world — I have built and refined investigative workflows leveraging blockchain analytics, advanced SQL, and AI tooling to surface complex money laundering schemes, fraud rings, and terrorist financing networks. I have exercised independent judgment on high-stakes cases and filed Suspicious Activity Reports to FinCEN when warranted. I have also served as an informal subject matter expert for team members, providing guidance on complex typologies and AI tool adoption across the AML operations function.

Prior to Circle, I built my compliance foundation at WePay, operating within the JPMorgan Chase umbrella, where I managed 300+ alert reviews monthly and maintained flawless audit documentation. My career began at AML RightSource, where I executed compliance programs across multiple financial institutions simultaneously as a contract analyst.

Across every organization I have been part of, I have been recognized as a consistent top performer in both investigation production and quality. I am committed to the principle that the future of financial crime compliance is data-driven, AI-augmented, and proactive — and I bring that standard to every case and every team.
