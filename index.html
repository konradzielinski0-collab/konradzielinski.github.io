Profile

About Me

Where regulatory rigor meets analytical precision.

I'm a Senior AML/BSA Compliance professional with over five years of specialized experience uncovering financial crime across the digital asset and fintech landscape. My work sits at the crossroads of rigorous regulatory compliance and cutting-edge investigative technology.

At Circle — the issuer of USDC and one of the most closely regulated digital asset firms in the world — I've built and refined investigative workflows that leverage blockchain analytics platforms, advanced SQL, and AI tooling to surface complex money laundering schemes, fraud rings, and terrorist financing networks. I've exercised independent judgment on high-stakes cases and filed Suspicious Activity Reports to FinCEN when warranted.

Prior to Circle, I honed my foundation in payments compliance at WePay, operating within the JPMorgan Chase umbrella, where I managed 300+ alert reviews monthly and maintained flawless audit documentation. My career began at AML RightSource, where I delivered compliance programs across multiple financial institutions simultaneously.

Across every organization I've been a part of, I have been recognized as a consistent top performer in both investigation production and quality. I am committed to the idea that the future of financial crime compliance is data-driven, AI-augmented, and proactive — and I bring that standard to every case and every team.
